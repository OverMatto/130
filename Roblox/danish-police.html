[DK] - Kingdom of Denmark - Police Training Center and HQ (July 2018)

I was tasked with building a training center and HQ for Danish Police.

The interior is based on Blume HQ from Watch Dogs 2.

Scripting

The place features automated doors and gates and a semi-automatic obstacle course. I've also begun to script a ranking and jail system but unfortunately the group never launched.
